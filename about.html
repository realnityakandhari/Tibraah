Our Story – Tibraah

Celebrating Punjab’s Soul, Delivered to the World

Where It All Began

At Tibraah, we know the joy and the heartbreak of planning a wedding from far away. Our founder grew up watching the vibrant wedding traditions of Punjab — the phulkari embroidery, the shimmering bridal lehengas, the handmade jewelry passed from generation to generation. When friends abroad shared how hard it was to find authentic, well-made Punjabi wedding attire, the mission was born: to bring the heart of Punjab’s wedding craftsmanship to couples worldwide — with trust, care, and a personal touch.

Our Heritage

With a vendor network rooted in 50+ years of craftsmanship, Tibraah works with master artisans, trusted boutiques, and expert tailors across Punjab. Every vendor is carefully selected for authenticity, quality, and reliability — because we believe wedding outfits aren’t just clothes, they are heirlooms.

How We Work

    Personalized Curation: We listen to your vision, your style, and your budget. Trusted Vendor Network: Only vetted partners who meet our quality promise. Seamless Communication: From WhatsApp updates to quality check photos, you’re always in the loop. Worldwide Delivery: Whether you’re in Toronto, London, Sydney, or New York — Punjab comes to your doorstep.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: Every piece we send reflects the true craftsmanship of Punjab.
  • Care: Weddings are emotional — we treat every order like it’s for our own family.
  • Transparency: No surprises — you see what you’ll receive before it ships.

The Personal Touch

For couples abroad, wedding shopping in Punjab can feel impossible — navigating time zones, unreliable couriers, and uncertain quality. Tibraah is your eyes, ears, and heart in Punjab, ensuring you get not just a product, but a piece of tradition delivered with care.
