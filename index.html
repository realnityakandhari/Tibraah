FAQ

How does pricing & shipping work?

We quote item prices and an estimated shipping slab up front. Final shipping is billed at actuals with weight slip & tracking. For larger orders, we subsidise part of shipping—ask for active offers.

What if something doesn’t fit?

We size with measurements + buffer. If a minor alteration is needed, we contribute to local tailoring—case by case.

Do you keep inventory?

We operate a curated, on-demand model via trusted vendors. Once you approve & pay the advance, we block the exact pieces and share a QC video before packing.

Which countries do you ship to?

USA, Canada, UK, EU, Australia, NZ and more. Express & economy options are available.
